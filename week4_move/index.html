ABOUT Across the Spider-Verse

"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is an upcoming animated superhero film that acts as a direct sequel to the widely acclaimed "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" from 2018. This movie is part of the expanding Spider-Verse franchise and continues to delve into the narrative of multiple Spider-People originating from diverse dimensions. The storyline centers around Miles Morales, his reality's Spider-Man, as he embarks on new adventures. During his journey, he crosses paths with Gwen Stacy, also known as Spider-Woman or Spider-Gwen. Together, they navigate through the complexities of the multiverse, encountering potential variations of Spider-Man while engaging in battles against adversaries. The film promises to retain its distinct animation style, visually captivating audiences, and maintaining its innovative storytelling approach.
